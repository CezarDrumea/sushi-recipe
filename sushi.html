Sushi recipe

Description

An easy homemade sushi recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed aka nori
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  • 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado sliced
  • soy sauce for serving

Steps

  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat
  2. Cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice
  3. Smooth gently with the rice paddle
  4. Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice
  5. Roll it up tightly
  6. Slice with a sharp knife
  7. Enjoy with soy sauce
